About us

The decision to merge these companies into a diversified lifestyle conglomerate stems from a strategic synergy aiming to create a unique and comprehensive consumer experience. Starbucks contributes its global retail footprint and consumer engagement, DermTech brings cutting-edge skincare technology, and Gap provides a platform in the fashion retail space. The rationale is to capitalize on the strengths of each company, fostering a blend of coffee, skincare, and apparel that caters to a broad consumer base. This integration enhances market reach, encourages brand collaboration, and fortifies overall business resilience.

Mission Statement

Uniting the world one latte, one pair of jeans, and one glowing skin at a time! Where fashion meets flavor, and skincare meets sass. Together, Starbucks, Gap, and DermTech bring you the perfect blend of style, smiles, and skincare giggles.
